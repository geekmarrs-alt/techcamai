FAQ

Common questions

Is Community really free forever?
Yes. The Community tier is the open-source repo. It will always be free and self-hostable. There are no usage caps, no time limits, and no credit card required.
When will Pro launch?
No date confirmed. Pro is a planning-stage tier. The features listed are targets, not commitments. Sign up for updates on GitHub.
Does TECHCAMAI store my footage in the cloud?
No. In the Community tier, all footage, snapshots, and clips are stored on your local device (your Pi). Nothing leaves your network.
How many cameras can I connect?
No artificial limit in Community. Practical limits depend on your Pi's CPU and RAM. A Pi 4 with 4GB RAM handles several cameras comfortably.
Does it support my camera brand?
If your camera exposes an HTTP snapshot URL (ISAPI, CGI, MJPEG still frame) or an RTSP stream, it should work. Digest auth is supported. ONVIF formal discovery is planned.
Is there a hosted version?
Not yet. The current version is self-hosted only. A cloud-hosted option is planned for the Pro tier but has no release date.
